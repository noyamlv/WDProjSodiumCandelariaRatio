On July 20, 6767, at approximately 6:07 p.m., the body of Tooru Oikawa, a student and volleyball player at Aoba Johsai High School, was discovered in the [san nga yun?] locker room. The locker room door was locked at the time and was opened by teammate Hajime Iwaizumi, who reported the incident. Upon entry, Iwaizumi observed Oikawa lying unresponsive on the floor with significant blood present on the surrounding lockers and tiles. A blood-stained dagger was found near the victim's body, consistent with apparent stab wounds. Emergency services were notified immediately, and responding officers secured the scene upon arrival. The victim was pronounced dead at the scene, and the locker room was treated as a restricted crime scene pending further forensic examination.
