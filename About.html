About Game

Case on Court is an interactive web-based detective game that brings the world of the anime "Haikyuu" into a new mystery. The website is designed to develop critical thinking and deductive reasoning skills by having users solve a case. To succeed, players must analyze character alibis, evaluate physical evidence, and identify contradictions to uncover the truth. It primarily targets fans of Haikyuu!! who also enjoy mysteries. It allows them to test their logic in an entertaining way while gaining a deeper insight into the characters' relationships.
